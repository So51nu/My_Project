About Us

We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.

Through our innovative platform, we've created a seamless way to redistribute surplus food, reducing waste and nourishing lives. Join us in our mission to create a more sustainable and compassionate world, one meal at a time.

Connect & Share

Our platform brings together food donors and those in need, creating a community of caring individuals and organizations.

Reduce Waste

By redistributing excess food, we significantly reduce food waste and its environmental impact, promoting sustainability.

Community Impact

Together, we're building stronger, more resilient communities by ensuring that good food reaches those who need it most.
